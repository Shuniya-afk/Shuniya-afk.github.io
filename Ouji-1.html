Ouji

Silhouette

Ouji consists mostly of shorts and trousers rather than skirts. The style is meant to be a somewhat younf boy or childish looking style. Blouses consist of ruffles and long sleeves. Waist coats and vests are also very popular. Ouji can be worn like any other style such as sweet or gothic!

Accessories

Ouji is a very accesory heavy style. Things such as capes, monocules,brooches,bows, suspenders, and canes are very popular. Newsboy caps or top hats are very popular headwear.As for shoes usually loafers or lace up boots are the norm.Socks usually come under the knee but for sweet ouji they can be thigh high.
