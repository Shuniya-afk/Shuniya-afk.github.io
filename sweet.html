Sweet Lolita

Silhouette

Sweet Lolita is most known for its large cupcake like skirtx and prints full of sweet things such as candy, fruits,animals,hearts, and any other cute thing you csn think of. It is the most "childish" and cute style of Lolita.The dresses are usually very vibrant or pastel colors like pink,yellow,white,lanvender, and sax. Prints can range from things like rabbits and bears to parfaits and strawberries or even mermaids. There is even a print with laundry detergent on it, the possibilities are endless!The dresses themselves can come in many different cuts such as ones with higher necklines or ones made like overalls called a Salopette.For blouses they are usually simple compared to the dress with just frills and bows and come in plain colors such as white pink and lavender. Uusally they have peterpan or round collars.

Accessories

Sweet Lolita is very well known for its novelty purses,jewlery and headwear. Most of these come adorned in cakes and other sweets with the logo of the brand on them. Jelly style soft rings are also very popular. Large headbows are usually the headpiece of choise but some also wear bonnets, cute shaped hair clips and even fake plush animal ear. Another popular item in sweet lolita are plushie bags with the design of the brands mascot. For example Baby the Stars Shine Bright's mascot ,Usakumya, is usually made into bags. For socks usually sweet lolitas wear tights with the print of the dress on them or solid colored tights/stockings. Frilled ankle socks are very popular as well as lacy wrist cuffs when wearing short sleeve blouses. Shoes are usually pastel colored tea party shoes that match the dress.
