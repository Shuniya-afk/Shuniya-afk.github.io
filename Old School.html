Silhouette

Old school Lolita is lolita that is based on when lolita first came to be. There were no prints on the dresses, only solid colors with embroidary and lace. The colors of the dresses are usually light blue,white,black,or red.Shirring was also very popular in dresses too. Shirring is the gathering of elastic that makes dresses more stretchy but at the time it was just used for texture purposes.

Acessories

Old School Lolita is best known for its simple black or white knee length lace topped socks and rocking horse shoes. The headwear of choice is a thin square headdress.There are usually no jewlery or any other accessories worn.
