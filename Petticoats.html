Petticoats

There are three different types of petticoats; A-line, Cupcake, and Bell shaped. The chart below will descrube the differences between them. NOTE: Petticoats are not supposed to be seen when wearing lolita so when choosing your petticoat please choose one that is 5cm under the length of your dress!

  A-Line  poofs out more at the bottom than the top, looks like the letter A  mostly used in gothic lolita
 Cupcake  poofs out around the hips the most, looks like the icing on a cupcake   mostly used in sweet and very specific gothic coords
 Bell  just another name for a cupcake petticoat, refers to the bell like shape of the skirt when wearing
