Gothic Lolita

Silhouette

Gothic Lolita is meant to give off a very elegant,mature look compared to some of the other styles.In order to achive this most lolitas wear an A-line shapped petticoat to give their dresses a small amount of puff.The dresses themselves are still adorned with ribbons,lace and frills but the prints are usually darker themes such as coffins, churches,bats,and things of that nature.As for colors most dresses come in dark colors sucha as maroon,black,red, dark blue,white, and purple but recently brands have been releasing dresses in pink and other bright colors but still with a gothic twist. As for blouses, a very popular style of blouses are Hime sleeved blouses which are blouses with very large and wide sleeves( Atelier Pierrot is most known for this type of blouse).Other blouses consist of square necklines, lots of ruffles, jabots, and high necklines.

Accessories

Common Gothic lolita accessories are bonnets,square headdresses, Bows,chockers, and veils. As for leg wear usually knee length or over the knee length socks are worn with various edgy prints or lace.For shoes many lolitas wear platform pumps or mary janes. Rocking horse shoes are also very common as well as dark colored tea party shoes.Jewlwery is usually silver and gold with gems or crosses or other dark motifs attatched.
